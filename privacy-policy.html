Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026

Oort Technology ("Oort", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and the rights you have over it.

1Who we are

The controller responsible for your personal data is OORT CLOUD TECHNOLOGY S.L. ("Oort", "we", "us"), a company incorporated in Spain providing nearshore IT and project-management services.

For any privacy-related question or to exercise your rights, contact us at contact@oortcloud.tech.

2Information we collect

  • Contact details you give us — such as your name, email address, company and message — when you reach out to us.
  • Basic usage data your browser sends automatically, such as IP address, device and browser type, and pages visited.

3How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide our services.
  • To operate, maintain and improve our website.
  • To meet our legal and contractual obligations.

We do not sell your personal data, and we only share it with service providers who help us operate (for example, hosting or email), under appropriate confidentiality terms.

4Legal basis

Where the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, we process personal data on the basis of your consent, our legitimate interests in running our business, or to take steps at your request before entering into a contract.

5Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

6Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, object to or restrict its processing, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, email contact@oortcloud.tech.

7Cookies

Our website uses only strictly necessary cookies and similar local storage needed for it to function — including a small flag we store to remember that you've dismissed our cookie notice. We do not use analytics, advertising or other tracking cookies, so no consent is required for the cookies we set. You can still control or block cookies at any time through your browser settings.

For transparency, our pages load web fonts from Google Fonts. As part of serving those fonts, Google may receive your IP address. If you would prefer no third-party requests at all, we can self-host these fonts on request.

8Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

9Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at contact@oortcloud.tech.
